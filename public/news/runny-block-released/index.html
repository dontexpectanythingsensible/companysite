Runny Block released

13 March

Good news everyone! Runny Block, a colourful infinite runner game with more than twenty game-changing powerups, is now finished and can be played for free online or on Itch.io if that's your thing.

Runny Block also has touch support, so you can play it on your phone or tablet as well as online.

Have I mentioned that it's free? Go and play it!
