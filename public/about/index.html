Description

Don't Expect Anything Sensible is a one-man independent game development studio based in Bristol. It was set up in 2014, with the first release, 'Pairs', coming in early 2015 after a series of small web-only games.

'Runny Block', the second release, came in 2016, with a major overhaul released in early 2018.
